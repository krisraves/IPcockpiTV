sundial is a cozy front porch for two public, community-kept playlists — Free-TV/IPTV and iptv-org. It hosts no video; it just plays the free-to-air streams they point at. Previews are grabbed in your browser as you scroll and kept for a day.
Streams belong to other folks: some are far away, some drift offline. If one won't load, it's the stream, not you — drift to another. Check what's legal where you are.
