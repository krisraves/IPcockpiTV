SIGNAL  is a player frontend. It hosts no video — it reads two public, community‑maintained M3U playlists (Free‑TV/IPTV and iptv‑org) and plays the free‑to‑air streams they link to.
Streams are run by third parties: some are geo‑locked, some go offline without notice. If a channel won't load, it's the stream, not you — pick another. Check what's legal to watch where you live.
