sundial is a front end for two public, community-maintained playlists — Free-TV/IPTV and iptv-org. It hosts no video; it just plays the free-to-air streams they point at. Channels that won't load are dropped automatically. Streams belong to other people and may be geo-locked or go offline; if one won't play, it's the stream, not you. Check what's legal where you are.
