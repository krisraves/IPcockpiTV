SIGNAL is a console frontend — it hosts no video. It reads two public, community-maintained M3U playlists (Free-TV/IPTV and iptv-org) and plays the free-to-air streams they link to. Telemetry, presets, favorites and the monitor wall run entirely in your browser.
Streams belong to third parties: some are geo-locked, some go dark without notice. A channel that won't load is the stream, not you — surf on. Check what's legal to watch where you live.
